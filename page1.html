E-Kachra :-Smart        solutions for your e-waste

E-Waste Management :- Collection & Processing.
                                           -Reduce, Reuse & Recycle.

With the technology advancing at such a high rate, many electronic devices become "TRASH" after a few short years of use. With us generating about 40 million tons of e-waste every year, E-Waste is about to become the greatest problem the world has ever faced. 
We at E-Kachra aim to provide a solution for this very problem.

We at e-kachra are a team of passionate youths who aim to serve this planet better by reduing substantially the amount of e-waste being generated.

Collection

We will have collection centres for every city where individuals, kabadiwallas & scrap dealers can sell their e-waste.

Gain from E-Waste

We can fix a evaluation category for most types of e-waste. When a person will bring his/her e-waste, it will be evaluated and he/she will be paid accordingly.

Shipment

Once the collected e-waste has been segregated, it will be shipped to the central recycling facility (CRF) for further processing.

Sorting

 As the initial step, we will sort out the repairable and non-repairable appliances at the collection centres itself.

Dismantling

The first step which will help in further sorting of the e-waste, will be dismantling. In this process, we extract all the useful parts which can be reused again. The hazardous elements are also removed here. 

Recycling

Once we are done with the sorting and dismantling processes, we can send the e-waste to the CRF for recycling.

Central Recycling Facility
(CRF)

The collected e-waste will be shipped to the CRF directly from the collection centres where it will be segregated again, shredded and recycled into different products.

Sorting & Processing

The e-waste will be first dismantled and shredded into pieces less than 100 mm in size. Then it will undergo various separation methods like hydrometallurgy and electrochemical recycling to extract the metals. The metal ore procured from these processes can then be refined and reused.

Product

We recyle e-waste and convert them into different products which you can buy from our company.
